Ellen Main Tutoring

In-person tutoring for ages 5–10 • South Pasadena, California

About

Ellen Main is a highly experienced educator with over 30 years working in school systems as a teacher, tutor, and mentor. She holds a master’s degree in child education and brings a thoughtful, personalized approach to every student.

As both an educator and a parent, she understands how to support children in building confidence, curiosity, and strong foundational skills.

Approach

Sessions focus on developing fine motor skills, creative thinking, and hands-on learning—providing balance in a world increasingly dominated by screens and test-focused instruction.

Each session is tailored to the individual child, with an emphasis on patience, engagement, and long-term growth.
